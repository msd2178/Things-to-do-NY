About

"Things to Do! NY" is an event listing app that enables users to browse through the New York Times' database of events happening in New York. The users can search for events around the 5 boroughs of NYC and use the map feature to see the location of the event. The app lists the type of event, date and time, whether it is a NYTimes pick of the week and if it is a free event.

Users can bookmark events and go to the corresponding event page from the bookmark panel. Individual bookmarks are easily removed, and there is also an option to remove all bookmarks. Bookmarks are saved across sessions.

By Kira Ullman, Meril Dsouza, Sahana Subbanna, Yumeng Liao

Help

1. Choose the date range you want to search for events.
2. Choose the borough and radius of search.
3. If you wish, specify a specific event type
4. Click on the search button.

When the events get displayed, you can click on the markers on the map to see which event is located at the place and go to the event webpage.
You can see which events are free, which are chosen by NYTimes as pick of the week and date and time of the events.
Click the bookmark button for an event to add it to your list of bookmarks in the top right corner.
You can freely add and remove bookmarks, or even clear all of them.
There are buttons leading to the individual pages of each event you have bookmarked.
